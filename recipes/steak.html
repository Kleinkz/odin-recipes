Steak

Description

Steak is a dish made with usually beef,however, other types of red-meat can also be used. It isn't really hard to make and you do not have to be a chef to make delicious juicy flavour-full steak. So what are we waiting for? Let's get right into it!

Ingredients

  • Beef(amount depends on how much you would like to eat)
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Pat the steak dry
  2. Season it generously on both sides with salt and pepper. Do not leave the edges too!
  3. Heat your pan and wait for it to be really hot
  4. Then, add oil. Usually, two or three teaspoons should be enough
  5. Carefully, place the steak inside the pan and wait for a few minutes for it to be cooked(3-4 minutes should be enough)
  6. Flip it and do the same for the other side
  7. When it looks brown, add butter to enhance the taste
  8. Smear with butter for a few minutes and voila, your steak is ready!
